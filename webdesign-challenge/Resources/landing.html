    Summary

    The objective of this research was the relationship between latitude and different weather patterns. Open Weather API was used to randomly pull data from hundreds of different cities.

    Matplotlib and Pandas was use to analyze and graph the comparison of latitude to cloudiness, wind speed, humidity and max temperature.
